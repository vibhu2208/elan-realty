ABOUT ELAN GROUP

ELAN GROUP - THE TOP BUILDER

The Elan Group, one of the top real estate developers in Gurgaon, started in 2013 with a big, simple goal: to change how shopping malls and office buildings are made in India. They dreamed of creating new, better places where people could work, shop, and just hang out. They don't want to build the same old boring stuff; they want to bring fresh ideas and make spaces that are really exciting and useful for modern life.

This company is most trusted because they have built many huge, famous projects. You may hear names like Elan Epic, Elan Miracle, Elan Town Centre, and Elan Paradise. These buildings are proof that the Elan Builders care about high quality, new ideas, and doing things perfectly. They believe a building shouldn't just look good, it should be smart and connect people and places in a helpful way.

The Elan Group has a huge team of expert architects (who design the cool looks) and engineers (who make sure everything is safe and strong). These pros work hard to use only the best materials and technology. This allows them to create buildings that are not only safe and strong but also super stylish, places that people genuinely love to visit and use. Being a leading builder in Gurugram, Elan ensures every project matches global standards of design and innovation.

In just about eight years, the Elan Group has grown super fast and completely changed the way businesses build properties in India. They mix the latest technology with clever designs to create great places for both offices and shops. Some of their other popular projects include Elan Mark, Elan Presidential, and Elan Empire. The company plans to keep growing and always try new things. Their main focus is giving buyers and investors the best possible experience with modern, valuable spaces in the Gurgaon real estate market.

Elan Imperial – The First-Ever Luxury Mall of New Gurugram

Elan Imperial, New Gurugram's first ultra-luxury mall in Sector 82, spans over 9 acres and offers world-class commercial shops in Gurgaon, a state-of-the-art 4-screen multiplex, diverse food courts, and a premium five-star hotel. Designed by the renowned global firm Benoy, it features triple-height shops, double-height restaurants with terraces, high-speed escalators, 24/7 power backup, and multi-tier security for a seamless shopping and business experience.

Strategically located near Dwarka Expressway, NH-8, and opposite the luxury DLF Ultima project, it enjoys excellent connectivity to Cyber City, IGI Airport, and the upcoming Metro. Surrounded by a dense residential population of over 50,000 families, Elan Imperial Gurugram guarantees high footfall and lucrative business opportunities. Developed by the trusted Elan Group Gurgaon, known for quality and innovation, this project offers investors assured returns, prime retail shops, luxury amenities, ample parking, and profitable commercial investment opportunities (RERA No. 15 OF 2024).
