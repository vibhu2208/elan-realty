About Elan Imperial

Elan Imperial, Gurgaon's first-ever luxury mall, prominently located in the prime Sector 82. Spanning approximately 9 acres, this landmark commercial project seamlessly merges a grand shopping mall and a five-star hotel under one roof, establishing a unique destination for premium shopping, entertainment, and hospitality.

Elan Imperial is conceptualized as a high-return investment opportunity for those seeking the best commercial project in Gurgaon. Developed by the reputable Elan Group, known for its quality and trusted market presence, this project promises lucrative returns and enduring value. The blend of a luxury retail space and a 5-star hotel ensures a consistent stream of high-value footfall.

The mall offers a wide variety of retail spaces, a modern multiplex, a dedicated food court, and the luxurious hotel. With over 50,000 families living nearby and the prestigious DLF Ultima project just opposite, the location guarantees high customer visibility.

Strategically connected to major roadways like the Dwarka Expressway, NH-8, Golf Course Road Extension, and close to Cyber City, IGI Airport, and upcoming metro lines, Elan Imperial Sector 82 Gurgaon provides effortless access for both businesses and customers. This perfect combination of location, infrastructure, and amenities makes Elan Imperial an unbeatable choice for commercial investment.
