About Elan The Presidential

Elan The Presidential is a premium residential project by Elan Group, located in Sector 106 Gurgaon, right along the Dwarka Expressway. Spread across a large area, this luxury development offers modern 3 BHK, 4 BHK, and 5 BHK apartments designed with top-quality interiors and world-class facilities. Known as a landmark address in Gurgaon, Elan The Presidential 106 is perfect for families who want a modern lifestyle along with comfort and elegance.

Apart from quality living, Elan Presidential Sector 106 offers modern amenities such as a swimming pool, outdoor gym, amphitheatre, multipurpose hall, hassle-free parking, and landscaped gardens. Its location makes it even more attractive, as it connects easily to Delhi, IGI Airport (just 15 minutes away), top schools, hospitals, and major hubs of Gurgaon. With elegant design, excellent connectivity, and premium features, Elan The Presidential Gurgaon becomes a smart choice for both luxury living and investment.
