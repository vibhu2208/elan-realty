About Elan Paradise

Elan Paradise Sector 50 Gurgaon is a premium commercial project by the reputed Elan Group, strategically located in Nirvana Country. Spread across 4 acres, this development perfectly blends luxury retail, dining, and entertainment. It offers smartly designed retail spaces ranging from 300 to 1200 sq. ft., ideal for shop owners and investors seeking high returns.

Known as Gurgaon's first concept-based high-street retail in Sector 50, Elan Paradise delivers a modern shopping and leisure experience with futuristic architecture by UHA London. Situated in Sector 50 Gurgaon, Elan Paradise enjoys excellent connectivity to major points like NH-8, Golf Course Extension Road, Sohna Road, and IGI Airport, just 30 minutes away.

The project features a five-screen multiplex, spacious food courts, fine dining restaurants, bars, and pubs, making it a vibrant entertainment hub. With world-class amenities including 24/7 security, ample parking for 1200 cars, power backup, and central air conditioning, Elan Paradise Sector 50 promises a smooth experience for business and visitors alike.

Elan Paradise in Nirvana Country Gurgaon represents a futuristic commercial destination blending retail, entertainment, and hospitality. With possession expected by December 2024, it offers an excellent opportunity for investors and entrepreneurs to be part of one of Gurgaon's fastest-growing commercial hubs.
