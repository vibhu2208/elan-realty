THOUGHTFULLY DESIGNED RESIDENTIAL COMMUNITY

Elan Sohna Road in Sector 49, Gurgaon, is a thoughtfully designed residential community that blends comfort with modern living. Spread across 6.5 acres, it offers 5 elegant towers and 458 premium apartments. Homebuyers can choose from spacious 3.5 BHK and 4.5 BHK residences, complete with Italian marble flooring, modular kitchens, and smart air-conditioning for year-round ease. The project emphasizes open spaces, greenery, and a lifestyle that feels both luxurious and welcoming. With possession expected by September 2032, it gives families and investors ample time to plan for the future. The Elan Sohna Road price starts from ₹10.5 Cr to ₹12 Cr, making it an address that balances sophistication with long-term value in Gurgaon real estate.
